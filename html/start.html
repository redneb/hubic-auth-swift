This guide will help setup hubic-auth-swift for use with your hubiC account.
To start, login to your hubiC account and click on the account menu button at the top right of your screen.
Then select My account → Developers → Add an application.
Enter your last name and use as the redirection domain.
Click OK. The newly created application should appear on the list.
Locate the application in the list and click details.
